You need 
	- 4 eggs
	- 450ml milk
	- 250g flower
	- salt
	- 1 package of vanilla sugar

Put the flower in a bowl, add the eggs the salt and sugar and some of the milk.
Take a mixer and start mixing the ingredients while adding more and more milk.

If finshed put a pan on the stove add some oil into the pan wait until it is hot and then add some dough.
